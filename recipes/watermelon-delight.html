Watermelon Delight

Description

A cold watermelon drink that's perfect for hot days!

Prep: 5 mins

Servings: 2

Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • 5 ice cubes
  • 2 tablespoons honey

Steps

  1. Blend watermelon, ice cubes, and honey in a blender until smooth.
